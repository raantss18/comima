Two generations, one same love of mathematics

16 July 2026

On one side, students spontaneously gather around a problem, searching together, helping each other, without being asked to.

On the other, a university professor takes the time to sit down next to a student, bent over their notebook, to explain a notion.

Two generations, one same love of mathematics. Here, you come to improve, but above all to learn from one another.
