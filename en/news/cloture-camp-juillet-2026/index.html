Camp closing ceremony: prize-giving and farewells

17 July 2026

The Mathematical Olympiad Camp closed on the afternoon of Friday 17 July with a prize-giving ceremony.

Prize-giving

The national laureates were rewarded by region, at the end of the team and individual contests that ran throughout the week. Medals, certificates and congratulations for students from Antananarivo, Antsiranana and Ambositra.

Farewells

A tough week, high-level mathematics… and yet, when the time came to part, the students, coaches and organisers did not want to leave one another. The closing ended with smiles, joy — and a few tears of goodbye. Together we lived unforgettable moments: mathematics brought us together.

Thank you

A big thank you to every participant, from near and far; to Animath for its funding and pedagogical support; to AEMIA for its contributions; to the volunteer coaches, driven by their love of mathematics, expecting nothing in return. And a heartfelt thank you to the trainers and members of the COMIMa association.
