The **66th International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO 2025)** took place in
July 2025 in Sunshine Coast, Australia, with more than 100 participating
countries.

## The problems

The six problems of this edition — from the famous Problem 1 about "sunny
lines" to the formidable tiling Problem 6 — are archived in our
[contest papers library](/comima/en/papers/) (official French version), in
PDF and LaTeX formats.

## Madagascar's goal

COMIMa is working towards a future Malagasy delegation at international
olympiads. Motivated students are welcome to join us — see the
[Contact](/comima/en/contact/) page.
