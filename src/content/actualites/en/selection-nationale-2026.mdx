The **national selection test** will take place on Saturday 12 September
2026 in Antananarivo. Its goal is to select the students who will follow the
intensive olympiad preparation ahead of Pan-African and international
competitions.

## Format

- **4 problems**, each marked out of 7 points, as in the olympiads;
- **4 hours**, no calculator;
- Topics: algebra, geometry, number theory, combinatorics.

## How to prepare

Train with our [exercise library](/comima/en/resources/) and build your own
problem sets with the [paper generator](/comima/en/generator/). Past
international olympiad papers are available in the
[archives](/comima/en/papers/).
