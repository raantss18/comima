From 13 to 17 July 2026, COMIMa is organising a **mathematical olympiad
camp** gathering the best students from the regions involved.

On the programme:

- **student training** in olympiad problem-solving techniques;
- **practice sharing between supervisors**, and with a representative of the
  **Animath** association.

Follow the highlights of the camp in the [news section](/comima/en/news/):
individual contests, visits and camp life.
