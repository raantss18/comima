The **Mathematics and Computer Science Olympiad Club of Madagascar
(COMIMa)**, based at Lycée Moderne Ampefiloha in Antananarivo, is making its
partnership with [Animath](https://www.animath.fr), the French association
for the promotion of mathematics, official.

## What this partnership brings

- Access to Animath's teaching resources (courses, booklets, past papers);
- Support for running maths clubs and training camps;
- Networking with other French-speaking olympiad clubs;
- Stronger preparation for international competitions: the International
  Mathematical Olympiad (IMO), the Pan-African Mathematics Olympiad (PAMO),
  and computer science contests.

## What's next?

Weekly training sessions resume at Lycée Moderne Ampefiloha. The exercise
library on this site will grow every week — head to the
[Resources](/comima/en/resources/) page to start training.
