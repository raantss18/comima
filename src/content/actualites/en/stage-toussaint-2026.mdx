COMIMa is organising a **one-week intensive training camp** during the
autumn break, from 19 to 23 October 2026, at Lycée Moderne Ampefiloha.

## Programme

| Day | Morning | Afternoon |
| --- | ------- | --------- |
| Monday | Core strategies | Placement test |
| Tuesday | Number theory | Group problem solving |
| Wednesday | Geometry | Mock olympiad #1 |
| Thursday | Algebra & inequalities | Combinatorics |
| Friday | Mock olympiad #2 | Corrections and awards |

## Registration

The camp is **free** and open to middle school (from 4e) and high school
students. Register with the club supervisors or through the
[Contact](/comima/en/contact/) page.
