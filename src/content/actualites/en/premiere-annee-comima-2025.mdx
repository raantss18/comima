COMIMa has wrapped up its **very first year**. Throughout the year, the club
held sessions **every two weeks** at **Lycée Moderne Ampefiloha (LMA)**,
bringing together students from several Antananarivo high schools: **LMA**,
**LJJR**, **LJF** and **Saint-Michel Amparibe**.

## A successful first experience

Despite the difficulties — limited resources, an organisation to build from
scratch, timetables to reconcile — this first experience was a **success**.
Week after week, a core of motivated students formed around the joy of
searching, failing and trying again, until tasting the satisfaction of a
beautiful solution.

## What comes next

The path ahead is already set: **opening branches in other regions of
Madagascar**, so the adventure is no longer limited to the capital. The goal
remains the same — to train our best students to a level worthy of the great
international competitions: the **Pan-African Olympiad (PAMO)**, the
**International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)** and others.

Beyond the medals, COMIMa wants to pass on a mindset: the **taste for
challenge** in their favourite subject, mathematics.
