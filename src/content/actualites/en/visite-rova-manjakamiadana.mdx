Wednesday 15 July, in the afternoon.

Two and a half days of intensive maths, tough problems… and yet, the smiles
remain! The passion is shared between students and supervisors.

This afternoon, a visit to the Rova of Manjakamiadana. Coming from
Antsiranana, Ambositra and Antananarivo, our young mathematicians were
genuinely fascinated by Malagasy history 🇲🇬 Proof that curiosity knows no
limits!
